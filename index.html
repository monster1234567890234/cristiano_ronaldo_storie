About Cristiano

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time. His journey from a small island to becoming a global superstar is an inspiration to millions.

With an incredible work ethic, technical skill, and competitive spirit, Ronaldo has dominated football for nearly two decades. He has played for some of the world's biggest clubs including Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al Nassr.

His influence extends beyond the pitch, making him one of the most followed athletes on social media and a global icon in sports marketing.
